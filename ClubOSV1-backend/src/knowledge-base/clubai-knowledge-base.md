## Troubleshooting Procedures

### 1. FROZEN / BLACK SCREEN / GAME KEEPS RESTARTING
**Symptoms:** Screen is frozen, black, unresponsive, game restarts mid-round, "unexpected state" error, TMB error codes

**Steps the customer can try:**
1. On the keyboard, press the **Windows key** to get to the desktop
2. Look at the **taskbar at the bottom** — find the **green** and **orange TrackMan icons**
3. **Right-click each icon** and close them both
4. On the desktop, find and **double-click the orange TrackMan icon** to reopen it
5. **Wait about 1 minute** for it to fully load
6. Start a new round or resume from "My Activities"

**Important notes:**
- If they have a **TrackMan account**, they can resume their round from "My Activities" on the main screen
- If they were playing as Guest, round progress may be lost
- If the restart doesn't fix it, **escalate to human staff** — they can do a remote system reset
- If it keeps happening repeatedly, staff may **refund the booking** and/or offer a **free hour code**

### 2. SIDE SCREENS NOT WORKING / OFF
**Symptoms:** Wall TVs are off, side screens blank

**Steps:**
1. Look **under the center logo on the TV** for a power button
2. **Press it** — the TrackMan side-screen display should appear
3. If screens show content but not the game view, see "No View Selected" below

### 3. SIDE SCREENS SHOWING "NO VIEW SELECTED"
**Symptoms:** TVs are on but say "No View Selected"

**Steps:**
1. Do the **TrackMan restart** (close both icons, reopen orange one, wait 1 minute)
2. The side screens should connect once you **start a round or go to the range**
3. If still not working, escalate to human staff for a remote reset

### 4. BALL NOT PICKING UP / NOT REGISTERING
**Symptoms:** Shots not detected, ball not tracking

**Steps:**
1. Make sure you're using a **clean white ball only**
2. **Remove all other balls** from the hitting area (extra balls confuse the sensor)
3. If still not working, do the **TrackMan restart** (close both icons, reopen orange one, wait 1 minute)
4. Lost round progress can be recovered from "My Activities" on the main screen

### 5. TRACKMAN LOGIN ISSUES / QR CODE NOT WORKING
**Symptoms:** Can't log in, QR code won't scan, password wrong, locked out

**Steps:**
1. You do NOT need an account to play — select **"Guest"** on the screen to start immediately
2. Playing as Guest means stats and round progress won't be saved
3. For account issues, they can try resetting their password through the TrackMan app
4. Recommend creating a TrackMan account at home before their next visit (download the TrackMan app)

### 6. CAN'T GET INTO THE BUILDING
**Symptoms:** Door won't open, didn't receive access link, link doesn't work

**Steps:**
1. Access link is sent **by text and email when you book and pay** — check text messages and email spam folder
2. The door handle **does NOT turn** — after clicking the link, just **pull the door open**
3. If the link was sent to an old phone number, or they changed numbers, **escalate to human staff** to unlock remotely
4. If the door physically won't open (mechanical issue), **escalate to human staff immediately**

### 7. "DEVICE USAGE HAS EXPIRED" ERROR
**This requires human staff intervention.** The TrackMan license needs to be renewed by staff or the TrackMan rep. Apologize and escalate immediately.

## Common Questions & Quick Answers

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| WiFi password | **ClubGolf** |
| Club rentals? | Right-hand clubs available at all locations. Reserve when making your booking. **No left-hand clubs currently.** Customers can bring their own. |
| How long for 18 holes? | About **2 hours for 2 people**. Tip: increase the gimme/auto-putt area in settings to speed up putting. |
| How many people per bay? | **Max 4 people** per box |
| Can I bring food? | Limited food is OK (nothing messy). **No alcohol.** Leave the space clean. |
| How do I extend my booking? | Book the next time slot through the website. If the slot isn't showing, text us and staff can add it. |
| How do I save my round? | Create a **TrackMan account** (download the app, scan the QR code in the bay). You can resume rounds from "My Activities." |
| Overtime policy | Unbooked overtime is charged at **2x the regular rate**. Book an extension if you need more time. |
| Can I change my booking? | Staff can change bookings — provide your name, current booking time, and desired new time. |
| Balls provided? | Yes, **new balls are provided** at the location. Use only the provided balls (to protect the screens). |

## Compensation & Escalation Policies (HUMAN ONLY)

These actions require human staff judgment — AI should NEVER promise or execute these:
- **Refunding a booking** — staff decision based on severity
- **Issuing free hour codes** — staff has promo codes (e.g., www.clubhouse247golf.com/freehour with code)
- **Extending time for free** — staff can adjust bookings
- **Remote system reset** — staff can restart TrackMan remotely via admin tools
- **Unlocking doors remotely** — staff has remote access control
- **Changing/cancelling bookings** — staff handles through booking system
- **Gift card issues** — staff resolves through payment system

## Key Business Context

- Locations are **unstaffed** — customers are alone in the facility
- All support is **remote via text message** (and occasionally phone)
- **TrackMan** is the simulator software — most tech issues are TrackMan software bugs, not Clubhouse hardware
- The most common fix for almost everything is the **TrackMan restart** (close both icons, reopen orange one)
- When issues can't be resolved, the team is generous with **refunds and free hours** to keep customers happy
- The team's tone is: **friendly, casual, apologetic when things go wrong, quick to make it right**
